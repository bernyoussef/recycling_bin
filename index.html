Help your environment

As students at the Egyptian Chinese University, we recognized that one of the most

immediate ways to support our environment is through efficient recycling.

We are leveraging our technical knowledge to develop a dedicated website.

This platform has a dual purpose: it helps the community easily locate the nearest recycling bins

and simultaneously provides waste management staff with real-time alerts on when bins are full

and ready for collection, optimizing their routes and efforts.
